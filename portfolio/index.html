wizards and Warlocks

Videogame developed in C++. Shooter engineered to work on the lowest end computers. Cross-platform for Windows and GNU/linux. Using our own Net and Graphic engines(Travelers Ocular Engine). Obtained the maximum possible score as a Multimedia Engineering team project at University of Alicante (2017/2018).

King of the Jungle

Videogame developed for GNU/Linux platform, using C++, SFML graphics library and Box2D physics library. King of the Jungle is a local multiplayer 2D shooter. Matches are up to 4 players and AI can replace players if wanted. There are 2 different game modes: All vs All and Coin Collect.
